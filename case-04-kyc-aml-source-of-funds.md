### 📤 Agent Response

Dear Trader,

Thank you for contacting Customer Support regarding your recent wire deposit and account status. 

As a regulated financial brokerage, we operate under strict Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulations and financial compliance standards. These rules require us to periodically update client documentation and conduct **Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)** when cumulative deposits exceed standard thresholds.

Your temporary trading restriction is a standard automated risk mitigation control while our Compliance team reviews your high-value funding request.

To complete this review and fully restore your trading access, please upload **one** of the following documents verifying your Source of Funds via our **Secure Client Portal > Verification > Document Upload**:

* **Recent Bank Statement:** Showing the source account for the $15,000 transfer alongside your full name and recent account activity (last 3 months).
* **Proof of Income / Salary Slip:** Issued within the last 3 months.
* **Tax Return / Business Revenue Proof:** For self-employed accounts.

> *Note: For security reasons, please do not send financial documents directly via email reply. Always use the Secure Client Portal.*

Once uploaded, our Compliance Operations team typically completes reviews within **1–2 business hours**. We appreciate your cooperation in helping us maintain a safe and compliant trading ecosystem.

Best regards,  
**Compliance & Operations Support Team**
